Hi, I’m Chloee. UX/UI design drew me in because I love watching good design unfold. I am a fiber artist—knitting, spinning, and dyeing fiber—and I have always found a need to bring life to the world through hand made art. I have a particular passion for color theory and typography which drives the aesthetics of my work. I enjoy design challenges that allow me to take existing work and make it more intuitive and exciting to use. I am trained in Figma, but I’m a student of many systems and I find pleasure in the challenge of learning something new.
